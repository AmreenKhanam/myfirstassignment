About

story line

In Mumbai, India, beautiful middle-class Jhanvi (Ayesha Takia) falls for lifelong gangster Radhe (Salman Khan), though she has two other men vying for her affections: the older Sonu Gates and Inspector Talpade (Mahesh Manjrekar), Radhe's nemesis. But the inspector is both already married and knee-deep in the corruption and gang violence that rules Mumbai. Radhe, too, may not be entirely what he seems. Between a kidnapping and a violent shootout, love can be brutal indeed.
